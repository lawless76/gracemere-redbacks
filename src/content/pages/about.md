## Our Story

The Gracemere Redbacks Soccer Club has been part of the Gracemere community for many years, bringing together players of all ages and backgrounds who share a love of the beautiful game.

We are a volunteer-run club committed to developing soccer at the grassroots level in Gracemere and the surrounding region. Whether you're lacing up boots for the first time or you've been playing for decades, there's a place for you at the Redbacks.

## Our Values

**Community** — We believe soccer is at its best when it brings people together. Our club is a welcoming place for players, families, and supporters.

**Development** — We're committed to helping every player grow, both on and off the pitch. Our coaches focus on skill, teamwork, and a love of the game.

**Inclusion** — Everyone is welcome here. We are a diverse and inclusive club that celebrates what makes each member unique.

**Fun** — Soccer should be enjoyable. We work hard and compete with passion, but we never forget that the game should be fun.

## Our Home Ground

We train and play our home games at our ground in Gracemere. Facilities include:

- Full-size playing fields
- Junior training areas
- Clubhouse and canteen
- Changing facilities

## Get Involved

There are many ways to get involved with the Redbacks beyond playing. We're always looking for volunteers to help with coaching, administration, the canteen, ground maintenance, and event coordination.

If you'd like to join the club or find out how to get involved, [contact us](/contact) today.
