## Programs Overview

The Gracemere Redbacks offer a range of programs to suit players at every stage of their soccer journey. From tiny tots to senior competition, we have a place for everyone.

---

## Junior Development (Ages 5–12)

Our junior development program is designed to give young players a love of the game in a fun and supportive environment. We focus on:

- Basic ball skills and coordination
- Movement and physical literacy
- Teamwork and sportsmanship
- Age-appropriate small-sided games

**Training:** Saturday mornings during the season  
**Registration:** Contact us for current fees and availability

---

## Youth Teams (Ages 13–17)

Our youth teams compete in regional competitions and receive more structured coaching around tactics, fitness, and individual skill development. Players are placed in age-appropriate teams and train twice per week during the season.

**Training:** Tuesday and Thursday evenings  
**Competition:** Saturday afternoons

---

## Senior Competition

Our senior men's and women's teams compete in the local and regional league competitions. We welcome players of all ability levels and are always looking for new members to join the squad.

**Training:** Wednesday and Friday evenings  
**Competition:** Sunday mornings

---

## School Holiday Clinics

We run soccer clinics during school holidays for children aged 5–14. These sessions are a great way for kids to stay active and develop their skills in a relaxed, fun setting.

Watch our [News](/news) page for upcoming clinic dates.

---
